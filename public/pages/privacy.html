Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Chocolate & Art Show collects, uses, and safeguards information when you use our website and attend our events.

1. Information We Collect

- Contact information (such as name and email) when you submit forms or contact us
- Transaction details related to ticket purchases (handled by third‑party processors)
- Media submissions and related metadata when artists submit artwork
- Usage data (pages visited, device/browser info) via cookies or similar technologies

2. How We Use Information

- To operate the website and provide requested services
- To communicate about events, submissions, and updates
- To improve site performance, content, and user experience
- To comply with legal obligations

3. Cookies & Analytics

We may use cookies and analytics tools to understand site usage and improve our content. You can control cookies through your browser settings; some features may not function properly if cookies are disabled.

4. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers (e.g., ticketing, email, hosting) solely to deliver services on our behalf, and as required by law.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You may request to update or delete your information or opt out of communications. Contact us using the information on our Contact page.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our website and events are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. International Users

If you access the site from outside the U.S., you consent to the processing and transfer of your information in the U.S. and other countries where our service providers operate.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of the site after changes signifies acceptance.

10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Please reach out via our Contact page.
